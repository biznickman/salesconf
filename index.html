SalesConf

Earlier this year I was sitting on a call with a fellow entrepreneur who walked me through his exact sales process. He showed me how he pre-sold $40,000 worth of a product that didn't even exist!

Now, whether you think $40,000 is a lot, that really doesn't matter. The strategies that he shared have enabled me to improve my own sales dramatically.

As soon as I hung up the phone call with him, I realized how powerful it would be to get a group of entrepreneurs together to learn some of the latest sales strategies being implemented. That's when SalesConf was born.

What's happening at SalesConf?

I've personally reached out to some of the top software and SaaS sales strategists and practitioners that I know. They are responsible for over a billion dollars in sales. That's no joke!

They are going to walk attendees through their existing sales systems to enable you to take your sales process to the next level.

You also may have the chance to get on stage critiques of your existing sales process. Bottom line: this event is all about practical skills and strategies that you can put to work in your startup right away.

I look forward to seeing you there,
Nick O'Neill
